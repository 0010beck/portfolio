AN ACT OF CARE (2026)

An act of care is an interactive zine that explores attention as a form of devotion, where noticing can become an act of care. The work consists of images and text transferred onto canvas and embedded with interactive electronics (LilyPad coin cells, LEDs, conductive thread, buttons, conductive fabric). When activated, small points of light illuminate specific areas of each image, guiding the viewer’s gaze and creating an intimate encounter. It invites them to slow down and examine closely. The text acts as a quiet support, or a prompt, encouraging further reflection on each image, and each image works individually or as a collection, creating a tactile form of storytelling. The materials are semi-fragile, drawing from DIY and wearable technology practices, where touch and impermanence are essential. This further emphasizes that attention is an embodied practice, it’s an action that requires care, time, and presence.
